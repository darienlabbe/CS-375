Project: A House

Here I present my final project for the CS 375 Computer Graphics Course. In this project,
I have designed and assembled a 3D model that allows users to experience a semi-realistic
walk-through of that model. While developing this project, I have incorporated various
individual models sourced online to save time and resources. Nevertheless, I alone have
crafted the entire model and implemented it into this website using three.js.

I hope you enjoy!
